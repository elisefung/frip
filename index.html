frip

A mobile app that builds flexible itineraries for friends looking to adventure in a new city.

Start planning an adventure.

Set your travel destination or browse nearby adventures curated by real locals. Add your friends to your adventure so you can share ideas and interests.

Build smart and flexible itineraries.

Start laying out your itinierary by browsing from our collection of actitivies or searching for an event. Frip will suggest local favorites for each activity that are on the way to your next destination so you’re never going out of your way.

Plan together, travel together.

Frip helps friends collaborate in the planning process so everyone’s opinions are heard. Group voting lets everyone vote for their top picks of places to go without . Then the trip planner can finalize the itinerary.
